2Reflect started with a simple problem: teaching through screens often removes the natural flow of thinking out loud with a pen.

We built 2Reflect so you can draw, annotate, and explain in real time while sharing any app.

If you are an educator, coach, creator, or engineer who teaches others, this blog will share practical workflows and tips that keep your sessions visual and clear.
